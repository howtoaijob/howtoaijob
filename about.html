Our mission

We believe the AI training job market should be easy to navigate for everyone — not just people who already know where to look. Instead of checking dozens of company career pages every day, you can scan one place here and see what's currently open, roughly what it pays, and whether it suits your background.

We don't run any of these jobs ourselves and we're not affiliated with the companies listed. We track public hiring pages across the AI training industry and bring the details into one place. When you click through, you apply directly with that company on their own site — we're simply the map, not the destination.
